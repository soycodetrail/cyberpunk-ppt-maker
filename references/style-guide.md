# Cyberpunk PPT Style Guide

Use this visual system across every cover, slide image, and PPT page.

## Core Aesthetic

- Dark-mode neon cyberpunk tech.
- Pure black or near-black background.
- Ultra-fine grey tech grid or micro-mesh.
- Soft ambient glows in red, blue, purple, cyan, and orange.
- Hard-edged sans-serif typography.

## Color System

- Large title: gradient-like contrast or bold accent split between `orange`, `cyan`, `pink`, `purple`.
- Body text: white or near-white.
- Emphasis words: golden yellow or cyan.
- Borders and chips: orange, cyan, pink.

## Composition Rules

- Keep strong top-left or center title focus.
- Use one main visual punch per page.
- Use 2 to 4 information blocks, not dense paragraphs.
- Prefer angled accents, glowing chips, and outlined cards over flat rectangles.
- For PPT, keep text editable and rasterize only the background.

## Mandatory Effects

- Visible outer glow on titles, tags, and frame edges.
- High contrast between text and background.
- Slight depth from bloom and ambient light patches.
- Black-grid atmosphere on every page.

## Content Tone

- Titles should be short and punchy.
- Subtitles should sound declarative, not like a manual.
- Each page should carry one main idea and one mood.

## Things To Avoid

- White slides.
- Flat corporate blue templates.
- Rounded playful illustration style.
- Dense bullet walls.
- Rasterizing all text into one image when a user needs editable PPT.
